Channel Strategy
5-Channel Lead Generation
The #1 Secret — The Smart List
Use AI to identify homes with roofs 15 years or older. Segment by home value — target higher-value homes first. This list feeds every single channel below. Without the right data, no channel performs. Build this before everything else.
Channel 1
AI Call Center
Hire bilingual VAs (Nicaragua or Mexico-based) — they speak English and Spanish, which fits the roofing industry naturally. Load smart lists into Rantoro Dialer. Use the drone pitch script. Pay per appointment set, not per hour.
Channel 2
Facebook / Meta
5–10 videos per month. Three frameworks: Hidden Damage, Build Video, Testimonial. Use Meta's dynamic creative testing — upload 5 versions and let the algorithm pick the winner. Retarget 10-second viewers aggressively.
Channel 3
Google PPC + Maps
Higher intent — people searching are ready to act now. Expensive, so your response system must be flawless. Post photos to Google My Business after every job. Hand every closed customer your phone to leave a Maps review on the spot.
Channel 4
Canvassing Team
Commission-only setters at 3% of deal value — paid only on closes. Use active build sites as a canvas magnet: knock every neighbor's door. Virtual canvassers can cold-call AI lists remotely. Setter + closer model splits the roles cleanly.
Channel 5
Drone Pitch
Works over phone, text, and door-to-door. Send the homeowner photos without ever getting on their roof. No in-person meeting required. This lowers resistance dramatically. It's the easiest yes to get.
Call Center Script — Drone Pitch
"Hey, what's going on? This is [Name] with Crown Roofing. My drone detected damage on your roof and I just want to make sure you don't get left out. All your neighbors didn't take their hard-earned money out of their bank account or 401k — they got their roof paid for by the insurance. Let me give you a free quote."

Field Operations
Setter → Closer Sales Model
Role: Setter
Appointment Setters
Commission-only. Paid 3% of deal value on close only — not on every lead. Virtual setters cold-call AI lists. In-person canvassers knock doors at active build sites. This structure creates natural follow-through accountability: no close = no pay.
Role: Closer
Sales Reps / Closers
Receive pre-set appointments from the Rantoro system. Review AI call recordings before arriving at the home. Run the War Wagon model: fill vehicle with 2–4 reps, work appointment clusters by territory. Use drone inspection as opening tool.
Role: Virtual Closer
Virtual Sales Team
Work old leads that canvassers set but didn't close in person. Close over phone or video call. This role attracts top performers who prefer remote work. Lowers cost per acquisition significantly and gives you a second shot at every unclosed lead.
The War Wagon Approach
Load up your vehicle with reps every morning. Work appointment clusters by geography. One rep sets up the drone inspection while another closes from the previous stop. Push every unclosed appointment to the virtual team same evening. Every close triggers a Google review request before you leave the driveway.

Avoid These
What Not to Do
❌ Do Not Buy Shared Leads
HomeAdvisor, Angi, and lead brokers sell the same lead to 5+ companies simultaneously. You're in a race you can't win and paying for it. Own your lead source or don't bother.
❌ Do Not Run Google PPC Without Automation
Google leads are high-intent but expensive. If your response time is over 5 minutes, you will burn through budget with nothing to show. Build the system first, then turn on the spend.
❌ Do Not Scale Ads Without Follow-Up Built
More Facebook leads without a working 48-hour sequence just means more wasted spend. Speed to lead is the multiplier on every dollar you put into ads.
❌ Do Not Storm-Chase Only
Weather dependency is a lottery ticket, not a business. If you wait for hail, you can't keep people on payroll and you lose your team every slow season. Build the machine now.
❌ Do Not Skip Door-Knocking at Build Sites
An active build is the single best lead source in the neighborhood. Every neighbor who sees your crew is a warm lead. Not knocking those doors is money on the table you're walking past.
❌ Do Not Let Old Leads Go Untouched
Your cold lead list is a gold mine. A quarterly Virtual Town Hall converts leads you already paid for at near-zero marginal cost. Ignoring it is ignoring free revenue.
